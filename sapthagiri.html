Atex is the culmination of our high-tech development with application for creating light weight textile membrane structures that embrace a new contemporary freedom of form, lightness and ecological appeal.

Atex combines the high strength and non-combustability of glass fibre fabric with the longevity and resilience of silicone for use as external or internal textile membranes, curtains, canopies, awnings, solar4 cladding etc.

Silicone coated fabric have outstanding flexibility over a wide temperature range and block UV – B light, which is harmful to human, animals and plants, but transmit UV-A light, essential for plant growth. Atex is environmentally friendly, reusable and recyclable.

Atex is ideal wherever quality, strength, stability, resilience and fire resistance are required for today’s modern fabric building culture of spectacular shapes and state of the art architectural ingenuity.
